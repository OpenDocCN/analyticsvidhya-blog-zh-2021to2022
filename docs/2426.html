  • setup.sh(您可以复制并粘贴它)->
mkdir -p ~/.streamlit/echo "\
[general]\n\
email = \"your-email@domain.com\"\n\
" > ~/.streamlit/credentials.tomlecho "\
[server]\n\
headless = true\n\
enableCORS=false\n\
port = $PORT\n\
" > ~/.streamlit/config.toml
  • Procfile(您可以复制并粘贴这个)->
